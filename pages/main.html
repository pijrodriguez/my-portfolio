Follow Thru is a goal management system for clients that is both sophisticated and easy to learn. Health professionals can simply create personalized goals for each of their clients from this website, and they access and track their progress through a confidential app. You can monitor their progress from week to week through the apps intuitive and insightful graphs.
NOTE: This project has been passed along to another developer and further improvements to this project has been made.

Cryptocurrency Portfolio is a project that I did with 2 other members during our React Native course. This mobile application serves as a portfolio app to check your holdings on your account. It uses Bittrex API to fetch holdings from a users account. Another cool feature is our very own twitter feed that fetches the latest cryptocurrency related tweets.
